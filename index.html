About Me


I am an aspiring developer with a drive to create. When I was 14 I developed an interest in creating music. Using various editing applications I began with just digital music editing. With a background in cello I began teaching myself how to play guitar. I used my new found guitar skills to record live music and then upload it to my computer for further editing. Developing is simply another pathway for me to create and make an impact. My experiences have shaped me, and I want those experiences to be reflected in my editing and developing.
